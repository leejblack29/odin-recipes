Negroni

Description

A Negroni is a classic cocktail, made of equal parts gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari, commonly garnished with an orange peel. It is considered an ap√©ritif.

Ingredients

  • 1oz gin
  • 1oz Campari
  • 1oz sweet vermouth
  • An orange peel, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Add gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth to a mixing glass.
  2. Add ice and stir for 25-35 revolutions.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass with new ice.
  4. Express orange peel and garnish.
